Here is a categorized list of LispMe Language Elements (primitive procedures, procedures, special forms, globals and
constants). The categorization is still a work in progress.

This list should be applicable to most other SchemeLanguage implementations, with a few noteable exceptions. (one of them
being "macro", which may, or may not be defined in the scheme of your choice. The other exception being the lack of
hygenic macros). If you find a lisp-me or palm specific function/special form in the first section, please mark it (if it
is a language specific addition) or move it below (if it is a palm-specific addition).
